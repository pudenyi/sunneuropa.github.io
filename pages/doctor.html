About

Dr. Onuoha has an outstanding 22+ year experience. She has now opened her own practice in our sun city. Dr. Onuoha obtained her medical degree from the University of Nigeria in the year 1996 with brilliant grades. She subsequently immigrated to the United States and finished her Neurology residency at Washington University in St.Louis. Dr. Onuoha is an active member of the Southwest Association of Nigerian Physicians since 2016. She is practicing at Sun Neuro PA located at 10657 Vista Del Sol Dr., Suite F, El Paso TX. Dr. Onuoha has been awarded more than 9 times for brilliant work in the field of Neurology. She is an affiliated MD in many Hospitals. She is now accepting New patients!
